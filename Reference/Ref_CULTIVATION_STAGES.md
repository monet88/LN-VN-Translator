## Overview

Korean Murim novels use **cultivation stages** (경지/境地) to mark martial arts mastery. Unlike Chinese xianxia's rigid realm system, Korean Murim employs **descriptive titles** and **language intensity scaling**:

**Key Concepts:**
1. Realms indicate **mastery level**, not just power
2. Language becomes more **profound/poetic** at higher levels
3. Techniques gain **philosophical depth** with advancement
4. Speech formality **intensifies** with realm difference

### Foundational Stages (입문 단계)

| Korean | Hanja | English | Hán Việt | Description |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|-------------|
| 미입문 | 未入門 | Pre-entry | Vị Nhập Môn | No martial training |
| 초보 | 初步 | Beginner | Sơ bộ | Basic training, < 1 year |

**Language Characteristics:**
- Simple, direct descriptions
- Basic technique names: "검법" (sword technique)
- Casual speech acceptable among peers

**Example:**
```
초보: "이 검법은 어렵네." (This sword technique is hard.)
```

## Language Intensity Scaling

### Speech Formality by Realm Gap

| Realm Difference | Speech Level | Example |
|-----------------|--------------|---------|
| Same realm | 해요체 or 반말 | "형, 이것 좀 봐." (Bro, look at this.) |
| +1 realm | 해요체 (polite) | "선배님, 가르쳐 주세요." (Senior, please teach me.) |
| +2 realms | 합쇼체 (formal) | "고수님, 여쭙겠습니다." (Master, may I ask?) |
| +3+ realms | 극존칭 (extreme formal) | "대협, 소인이 배알합니다." (Great Hero, this lowly one pays respects.) |

### Technique Description Evolution

**Same Technique at Different Realms:**

| Realm | Description Style | Example (Plum Blossom Sword) |
|-------|-------------------|------------------------------|
| 입문 | Literal | "매화처럼 빠른 검." (Sword fast like plum blossoms.) |
| 대성 | Metaphorical | "검이 만개한 매화를 수놓는다." (Sword embroiders blooming plum blossoms.) |
| 화경 | Philosophical | "검에 매화의 도가 깃든다." (The sword embodies the Way of plum blossoms.) |
| 현경 | Mystical | "검과 매화가 하나가 되어, 봄을 부른다." (Sword and plum blossom unite, calling forth spring.) |


**Major Breakthrough (대돌파):**
```korean
"천지의 기운이 단전으로 몰려들며, 새로운 경지가 열렸다."
```

```korean
"순간, 모든 것이 명료해졌다. 검의 도가 눈앞에 펼쳐진 것이다."
(In that moment, everything became clear. The Way of the Sword unfolded before him.)
```

## Language Examples by Realm

### Beginner (입문) - Direct Language

**Combat:**
```korean
"검을 휘둘렀다. 적이 쓰러졌다."
(Swung the sword. The enemy fell.)
```

**Training:**
```korean
"오늘도 백 번 베기 연습을 했다."
(Today I also practiced 100 sword swings.)
```

### Intermediate (화경) - Refined Language

**Combat:**
```korean
"검이 흐르듯 움직이며, 적의 허점을 찔렀다."
(The sword moved like flowing water, piercing the enemy's opening.)
```

**Training:**
```korean
"검과 하나가 되는 경지를 느낀다."
(I sense the realm of becoming one with the sword.)
```

### Advanced (현경) - Philosophical Language

**Combat:**
```korean
"검이 허공을 가르자, 세계가 둘로 나뉘었다."
(As the sword split the void, the world divided in two.)
```

**Training:**
```korean
"검의 도를 깨닫는 순간, 천지의 이치가 명료해졌다."
(The moment I understood the Way of the Sword, the principles of heaven and earth became clear.)
```

### Supreme (천하제일) - Mythical Language

**Combat:**
```korean
"그의 일검에, 하늘이 울고 땅이 갈라졌다."
(With his one sword strike, the heavens wept and the earth split.)
```

**Presence:**
```korean
"그가 서 있는 것만으로, 세상이 숨을 죽인다."
(Merely by his standing, the world holds its breath.)
```

## Translation Guidelines

### Realm Names: Keep Korean or Translate?

**Keep Korean in Dialogue:**
```vietnamese
"Sahyeong, anh đã đạt tới Hwa-gyeong (Hóa Cảnh) rồi sao?"
```

**Translate in Narration:**
```vietnamese
Anh ấy đã đạt tới Hóa Cảnh (境地化), cảnh giới mà kỹ năng trở nên bản năng.
```

### Power Descriptions: Intensity Matters

**Low Realm:**
```vietnamese
Simple: "Nội công của hắn rất mạnh." (His internal energy is very strong.)
```

**High Realm:**
```vietnamese
Poetic: "Nội công của ngài thâm sâu như biển, bao la như trời."
(His internal energy is deep as the ocean, vast as the sky.)
```

### Breakthrough Scenes: Build Tension

**Structure:**
1. Struggle
2. Insight moment
3. Energy surge
4. Realm confirmation

**Example:**
```vietnamese
Hắn vật lộn với giới hạn cũ, máu chảy từ miệng. (Struggle)
Đột nhiên, một tia sáng lóe lên trong tâm trí. (Insight)
Thiên địa chi khí ùa vào đan điền! (Energy surge)
"Hóa Cảnh... ta đã đạt tới Hóa Cảnh!" (Confirmation)
```
