## Anti-Translationese Patterns

### Core Principle
**Source rhythm > Target smoothness**

### 10 Cross-Category Patterns

1. **Resist Smoothing Impulse**
   - Problem: Vietnamese grammar wants to smooth fragments
   - Solution: Preserve fragment structure even if incomplete

2. **Avoid Over-Explanation**
   - Problem: Adding clarifiers Korean doesn't have
   - Solution: Trust context, match Korean brevity

3. **Lock Technical Terms**
   - Problem: Synonyms break consistency
   - Solution: Glossary = law, no creative variation

4. **Character Voice > Grammar**
   - Problem: Correcting "incorrect" casual speech
   - Solution: Preserve character speech patterns

5. **Korean Flavor Retention**
   - Problem: Over-localizing to Vietnamese
   - Solution: Selective retention for genre authenticity

6. **Formality Asymmetry**
   - Problem: Making pronouns reciprocal
   - Solution: Hierarchy encoded in pronouns

7. **No Arabic Numerals in HÃ¡n Viá»‡t**
   - Problem: Mixing numbering systems
   - Solution: Full HÃ¡n Viá»‡t for all classical terms

8. **Preserve Repetition**
   - Problem: Eliminating "redundant" repeats
   - Solution: Repetition = emphasis, keep it

9. **Dynamic > Static Verbs**
   - Problem: Using passive/static verbs for active scenes
   - Solution: Active voice, dynamic verbs for energy

10. **Context-Sensitive Capitalization**
    - Problem: Inconsistent proper noun treatment
    - Solution: Specific acupoints capitalized, generic lowercase
